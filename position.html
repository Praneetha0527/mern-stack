Amazon is a multinational technology company founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994. It began as an online bookstore but quickly expanded to offer a vast range of products and services. Known for its fast delivery through Amazon Prime, the company has revolutionized the e-commerce industry. In addition to retail, Amazon is a global leader in cloud computing with its Amazon Web Services (AWS) platform. Today, it stands as one of the most influential and valuable companies in the world.
